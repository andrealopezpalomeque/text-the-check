Receipt with Chat Inside

The receipt shape stays exactly as it is. Inside it: small abstract chat lines that say "messaging is happening here." Receipt = check. Lines inside = text.

The receipt is the container. The chat lines are the content. Together they tell the whole story: you're texting your finances.

Chat Lines Inside

Different densities and positions of the abstract chat lines

3 lines
Left-right-left

Classic chat pattern. Three lines alternating sides, clearly reads as a conversation.

2 lines
Just two

Even more minimal. One sent, one received. The simplest conversation.

4 lines
Full chat

Four lines — a real back-and-forth. Might be too busy at small sizes.

Compact
Compact 3

Tighter spacing, smaller bubbles. Cleaner at small sizes while still reading as chat.
